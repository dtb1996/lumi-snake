Lumi-Snake

Press Enter or Click the viewport to begin

Score: 0000

Controls

Movement: Arrow Keys, WASD
Pause: P
Mute: M
Restart Game: R
Exit to Menu: Esc

Description

In this game you control a rare bioluminecscent snake breed, also known as the Lumi-Snake. The catch? The snake's visibility diminshes over time and the only way to replenish it is by eating food that is scattered around the level. Keep in mind that the snake is still moving even if it becomes invisible, so be sure to eat food quickly and avoid fading into the darkness.
